The entry rule is the trigger. A contact enters once, when it first becomes true.
When an event happens A contact performs an action you track
When they join a segment A contact becomes a member of a segment
Yes Everyone already in the segment enters at launch, plus anyone who joins later
No Only contacts who join the segment after launch will enter
No — once per contact A contact goes through this automation only once, even if they meet the entry rule again later
Yes — can re-enter After they exit, a contact can enter again the next time they meet the entry rule
The exit rule decides when a contact stops. When it’s met, the remaining messages aren’t sent to that contact.
Your workflow has a Repeat step, so it loops. Pick an exit other than “finishing the workflow”, otherwise contacts would never stop.
When they finish the workflow Reaches the end of the chain — the default
